Getting to Erie, PA


There are many ways to get in and out of Erie. As the map shows, one can enter into Erie through two main interstates, I-90 from the east and west and I-79 from the south. Also, Erie includes an airport for those coming from longer distances and prefer not to fly. A link to the airport is included down below.
