Experience It All At The Milcreek Mall!

The Milcreek Mall Complex is located at 5800 Peach Street and has many stores within it. It offers many brands from sports brand Nike to casual wear such as American Eagle. This allows an enjoyable experience where everyone in the family gets what they want. The Milcreek Mall also has a food court that is packed with many different types of ethnic cuisine from Asian to Italian to American.
