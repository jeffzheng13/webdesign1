Erie's History

Erie, PA (Est. 1795)

Erie, PA has been around since the beginning of the United States of America. In being one of the first settlements, Erie is rich with history. For starters, the US Brig Niagara was the saving grace against the powerful British Navy in the War of 1812 and successfully defended Lake Erie. In addition, the Niagara is usually open for tours in the summer.

Watson-Curtze Mansion

Next up is the Watson-Curtze Mansion. The mansion is historic for its elegance and design. It is symbolic of Erie's wealth during the Victorian Era (c. 1891) and the wealth of Erie's industrialists. The attention to detail is immaculate. From the elaborate woodwork to the marble fireplaces to the mosaics, the mansion is truly one for the wealthy.
