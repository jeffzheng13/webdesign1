Erie Tourism

Erie, located in northwestern Pennsylvania on Lake Erie, is home to roughly 99,000 people. Erie consists of many surrounding towns and is well-known for its tourism and rich history. Some of these include the US-ranked Presque Isle Beach, US Brig Niagara, and Splash Lagoon.
